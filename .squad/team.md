## Project Description

**virtual-overlay-for-mac** — a macOS app that renders a persistent, ambient, text-only watermark identifying the current macOS Space (e.g. PRODUCTION, DEMO, CODING). Removes the cognitive cost of "where am I?" when working across many Spaces, monitors, fullscreen apps, and presentations. Out of scope for v1: window management, Mission Control automation, layout restoration, tiling.

**Stack:** Swift, SwiftUI + AppKit integration.
