## Summary

Susan joined the team as Designer and immediately shipped a programmatic app icon that matches the product's ambient, infrastructural aesthetic. The icon is a restrained typographic `[V]` mark (SF Pro ultra-light centered V framed by geometric bracket rules) on a near-black squircle.

## Design Philosophy Validated

- Icon echoes the product's core: ambient labeling system (like Bloomberg terminal, NYC subway signage)
- No gradients, neon, RGB, skeuomorphism, or decorative badges
- At small sizes (16×16), the V silhouette + bracket stems survive as an orientation mark
- Source-of-truth in code (the generator), not a design tool binary
